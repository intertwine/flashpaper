THE BIG IDEA
Replace recurrence and convolutions entirely with attention mechanisms.
The Transformer achieves 28.4 BLEU on WMT 2014 English-to-German (improving over best ensembles) while being 8× faster to train and 3× faster at inference.
ARCHITECTURE

The Transformer

The model is an encoder-decoder stack. Each layer has a multi-head self-attention sub-layer followed by a feed-forward network. Residual connections and layer-norm surround each sub-layer.
