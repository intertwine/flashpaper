Adam: A Method for Stochastic Optimization

Kingma & Ba — the optimizer you are almost certainly using right now.

WHY IT MATTERS
Combines the best of momentum (fast travel) and RMSProp (per-parameter scaling).
Adam adapts the learning rate for each parameter using estimates of first and second moments of the gradients.
THE UPDATE RULES
mₜ = β₁ mₜ₋₁ + (1−β₁) gₜ
vₜ = β₂ vₜ₋₁ + (1−β₂) gₜ²
θₜ = θₜ₋₁ − α · mₜ / (√vₜ + ε)
